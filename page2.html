1. Neon Street Mural Discovery

Source: Random unsplash search for street art (neon)

Neon street murals are becoming a city-night signature. This style uses fluorescent tones and bold geometric forms to evoke kinetic motion, typically painted across old brick walls.

2. Analog Brush Layers

Source: Unsplash acrylic textures, painterly technique

Artistic layering is about letting previous brush strokes remain partially visible. Transparency over texture fosters depth and invites the viewer to decipher hidden patterns.

3. Glitch & Generative Art

Source: Unsplash abstract gradients

Generative art bridges data and visuals. In this piece, algorithmic color distribution and flowing curves create a modern visual impression of motion and depth.
